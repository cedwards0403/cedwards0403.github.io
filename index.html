Clair Obscur: Expedition 33

Clair Obscur: Expedition 33 is one of the most recently released games of 2025. The game was made by a small indie team named Sandfall Interactive.

The game became extremely popular within multiple communities due to its unique blend of Traditional Turn-Based and Real-Time battle mechanics. On top of this, the story of the game was what also brought people when found online.

In a mysterious and ever fading world, what's left of France remains in the city of Lumiere, you play as a man named Gustave. Every year, a giant mysterious woman across The Continent paints a number on The Monolith. The number she paints goes down by one. And all those who are that age or older are erased. You play as a man named Gustave. And you are a part of the Expedition 33 to cross The Continent take down The Paintress so she can never steal any more futures!!!
